# Key Insights: Trader Behavior vs Market Sentiment

## Objective
Analyze how market sentiment (Fear/Greed) impacts trader behavior and profitability.

## Major Findings

### 1. Sentiment impacts profitability
Traders tend to perform better during Greed periods while Fear periods show higher volatility and losses.

### 2. Leverage drives risk
High leverage trades generate higher profits but also larger drawdowns during Fear markets.

### 3. Behavioral shift
Trade frequency and position size increase significantly during Greed sentiment.

### 4. Trader segmentation
High-frequency traders show more consistent performance than low-frequency traders.

## Strategy Recommendations

1. Reduce leverage during Fear markets  
2. Increase trade activity during Greed periods  
3. Avoid overtrading in extreme volatility  
4. Use risk-controlled position sizing  

## Bonus
A machine learning model was built to predict trade profitability using sentiment, leverage, and trade size.
